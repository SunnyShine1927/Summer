How Shooting Games Came To Be

First-person shooting games have been around for nearly 50 years. The first attempt at an FPS game was in 1973 with Maze War being installed at the NASA Ames Research Center. Players could move through a 3D maze slowly, while also shooting other players who appeared as eyeballs. Then jumping to the late 90's the game Duke Nukem 3D, was the first FPS that won acclaim for its highly interactive environments, adrenaline-filled gameplay, the way it made the protagonist a star, and the wise jokes the player made while he fought aliens. Now FPS games have become a staple subgenre of games. You can play with friends and the graphics have gotten so much better, that make you feel like you are in the game.

Call of Duty

Call of Duty is a video game series and media franchise published by Activision, starting in 2003. The games were first developed by Infinity Ward, then by Treyarch and Sledgehammer Games. Several spin-offs and handheld games were made by other developers. Call of Duty combined challenging single-player missions with a multiplayer. The earliest year the game goes back to is 1940, but most of the story occurs between 1944 and 1945. The game follows Private Ronald "Red" Daniels and his Infantry squad as they try to end the Nazis towards the conclusion of World War 2

Apex

Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale-hero shooter game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts. Players engage in team battles across a variety of locations while searching for supplies and weapons, including machine guns, rifles, and explosives. The map area decreases over time, forcing teams closer and closer to each other until only one team remains. There are skins players can unlock or buy for the characters you can play as. Apex Legends has earned over $1.3 Billion from Steam in just 2023.

Rainbow Six Siege

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is an online tactical shooter video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. It follows a secret international counterterrorist organization called "Rainbow". Its a realistic, tactical team-based shooter game where you needto do a lot of planning and execution. It's a 5v5 attack vs. defense gameplay with intense close-quarters combat in destructible environments. The First release of Rainbow Six was August 21, 1998 and then Rainbox six siege released in December 1, 2015 with better graphics.
