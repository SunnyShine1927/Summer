Stickers Are For Everyone

What inspired me to start making stickers

Hi there! I’m the artist behind Myrias DIYs 🎨✨ I’ve been drawing ever since I was a kid — always doodling cute things and dreaming up little worlds filled with color and happiness. Over the years, I took art classes on and off, learning new ways to bring my imagination to life. Eventually, I decided to take a big step — I left my retail job to focus on college and to start my own small business doing what I love most: creating art that makes people smile. 💖 Every sticker, keychain, shirt, bag, and bookmark I design comes straight from the heart. My goal is simple — to bring a little bit of joy and comfort back into people’s lives through cute, meaningful art. 🌈✨
