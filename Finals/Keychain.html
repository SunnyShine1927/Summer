How Vr Gaming Started

At the start of VR in 1968, Ivan Sutherland introduced the world to the first head-mounted augmented reality display, called the "The Sword of Damocles." which led groundbreaking foundation for the virtual reality devices we use today. In the 1980s and 1990s, companies like Sega and Nintendo started developing VR gaming systems. But VR became the most popular in 2010 because companies like Oculus and HTC Vive started making VR headsets that leveraged powerful graphics and motion tracking technology. You can grab things in the game using your own hand with the new VR equipment and can make people feel like they are really in the game they are playing.
